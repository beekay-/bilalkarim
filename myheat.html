MyHEAT

Helping millions visualize home heat loss for improved comfort, savings, and energy efficiency.

Visit Site

Problem

Existing energy efficiency solutions don’t motivate homeowners to make improvements. As a homeowner, it’s hard to fix a problem you can’t see.

Solution

Create a responsive web application with Heat Loss Maps and Ratings at its core to visually reveal inefficiencies inside and around homes.

Objective

Engage and educate homeowners with their home’s heat loss, and connect them with local service providers and incentives for improving the comfort, savings, and energy efficiency of their homes.
